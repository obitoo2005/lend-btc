How It Works

Deposit & Earn
Deposit BTC, MOTO, or PILL into the protocol and receive proportional shares. Your shares appreciate automatically as borrowers pay interest — no claiming required.
Borrow
Use your deposits as collateral to borrow other assets. BTC collateral unlocks MOTO/PILL borrowing. Maintain a Health Factor above 1.2 to avoid liquidation.
PILL Protection
Stake PILL tokens to lower your liquidation threshold from 1.2x to 1.1x. This gives your position a 10% safety buffer, letting you borrow more with less liquidation risk.
Yield Loop
Amplify BTC yield by looping: deposit BTC → borrow MOTO → relayer swaps to BTC → re-deposit. Up to 3x leverage. Higher yield comes with higher liquidation risk.
